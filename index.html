Masatoshi Yoshikawa received B.E., M.E. and Ph.D. degrees from Department of Information Science, Kyoto University in 1980, 1982 and 1985, respectively. He was on the faculty of Kyoto Sangyo University from 1985 until 1993. From 1989 to 1990, he was a visiting scientist at Computer Science Department, University of Southern California. In 1993, he joined Nara Institute of Science and Technology (NAIST) as a faculty member. From April 1996 to January 1997, he has stayed at Department of Computer Science, University of Waterloo as a visiting associate professor. From June 2002 to March 2006, he served as a professor at Nagoya University. From April 2006 to March 2023, he served as a professor at Kyoto University. From April 2023, he has been a professor at Osaka Seikei University.
